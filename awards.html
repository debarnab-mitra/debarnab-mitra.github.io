  • Best Poster Award (June 2021)
    Our poster titled "Concentrated Stopping Set Design for Coded Merkle Tree: Improving Security Against Data Availability Attacks in Blockchain Systems" won the best poster award at the IEEE North American School of Information Theory.
    [Poster]

  • Distinguished Masters Thesis Award (2019-20)
    I am the recipient of the 2019-2020 Distinguished Masters Thesis Award in Signals and Systems from the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at UCLA.

  • Guru Krupa Fellowship (2019-20, 2020-21)
    I am the recipient of the Guru Krupa Fellowship by the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at UCLA.

    I received an honorarium award from IIT Bombay for exemplary work done towards the project titled "Non Linear Junction Detector".

  • All India Rank 272 in JEE Mains 2014 (out of 1,400,000 candidates).
